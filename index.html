AKSHAYA PATHRA FLAG-OFF CEREMONY

BANK OF BARODA SMART INVEST
An animated infographic video introducing the benefits of digital investments with the launch of Bank of Baroda’s SMART INVEST app.
BOB 116 FOUNDATION DAY INVITATION
A reaffirmation of Bank of Baroda’s commitment and responsibility towards a better future with Baroda Earth, an initiative to Protect, Preserve and Conserve the environment on its 116th Foundation Day Celebrations.
COLLABORATION OF INDIANOIL WITH INDORE CENTRAL JAIL FOR REHABILITATION OF JAIL INMATES
A film tracing the transformative journey of jail inmates of Indore Central Jail, Madhya Pradesh as they contribute to social work during their serving time.
EXCELLENCE OF SHRI V.SATHISH KUMAR. INDIANOIL
A visual photo album of Mr V Satish Kumar on his 1st anniversary as the Director (Marketing) at Indian Oil Corporation.
INDANE PRADHAN MANTRI UJJWALA YOJANA BENEFICIARY
A documentation from the Indane Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana Beneficiary an initiative by the Government of India Series capturing the relief of the consumers as they shift from strenuous use of wood for cooking to using Indane LPG.
INDANE SURAKSHA LPG HOSE
An informative AV with an immersing blend of 2D Animation and voice-over on Indane Day, acknowledging 1 Crore LPG hoses installed in the previous 6 months by IndianOil.
INDIANOIL 77TH INDEPENDENCE DAY CELEBRATIONS

A stirring visual ground report of the flood-affected area of Chiplun in 2021, capturing the turmoil of survivors and their hardships, and IndianOil’s support in providing relief resources to the masses.

A capsule of memories as IndianOil bids farewell to Shri D R Paranjape, Executive Director I/c Finance, after his illustrious journey of success and growth with the Corporation.

A CSR initiative by Indian Oil Corporation urging its employees to impart education to underprivileged children of domestic helpers.
