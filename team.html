Meet the Team
We are a team of fully equipped professionals
from varied streams and skillset.
UJJAWAL DUBEY
Founder, Director
Documentary Filmmaker & Photographer holds a Masters Degree in the field of Arts from The University of Arts London, United Kingdom with an industry experience of over a decade, Ujjawal has also earned a BFA in Photography and Visual Communication as a Gold medallist from Jawaharlal Nehru Architecture and Fine Arts University Hyderabad, India. His work has been published in both International & National Digital Space.

SHIVANGI SINGH
Co-founder, Screenwriter, Director
Shivangi is a multifaceted artist with a passion for storytelling. A graduate of the University of Delhi's Gargi College, Shivangi is a screenwriter and director who thrives on the unconventional. Beyond her academic pursuits, she's immersed herself in theatre, delved into Hindustani classical music, and wears many hats as a photographer, editor and graphic designer. She has contributed several articles to Feminism in India and has served as the Research and Writing Head at the United Nations Foundation's Girl Up India.
